Remove Yourself from the Friend Queue

The purpose of this page is for friends currently on the friend queue to be able to remove themslves from the queue.

You can add yourself as many times as you want, though be aware that the number of times you add yourself to the list is the number of people you are willing to help.

Pressing the remove button will remove all instances of your name from the queue. This lets us know that you are too busy to be able to help at the moment and at any point you are able to re-add yourself to the queue by going back to the add page.
